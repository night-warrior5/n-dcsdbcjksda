SAT Math Practice Test

Module 1: No Calculator

1. A group of students voted on five after-school activities. The bar graph shows the number of students who voted for each activity. How many students chose activity 3?

2. What percentage of 300 is 75?

3. Solve for x: 2x = 36/25. What is a solution to the equation?

4. Three more than 8 times a number x is equal to 83. Which equation represents this situation?

5. Hana deposited a fixed amount into her bank account each month. The function f(t)=100+25t gives the amount in dollars after t deposits. What is the best interpretation of 25?

6. A customer spent $27 to purchase oranges at $3 per pound. How many pounds of oranges did the customer purchase?

7. Nasir bought 9 storage bins at the same price. After using a $63 coupon, the total cost was $27. What was the original price for one bin?

Module 2: Calculator

1. Based on the line graph of chipmunk counts from 1989–1999, in which year was the number greatest?

2. A fish swam 5,104 yards. How many miles did it swim? (1 mile = 1,760 yards)

3. Which expression is equivalent to 3·12x³ − 5x?
